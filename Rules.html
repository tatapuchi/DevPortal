Rules

Basic rules for our network, so that the staff don't have to do as much moderation

Spam

Spam is generally not appreciated, and can results in kicks or bans. Our guidlines for what counts as spam is as follows:

  • Multiposts, in the same or numerous channels. This includes:
    • Questions
    • Copypasta
    • Advertisements
    • Unnecessary pings
    • Unwanted Conversation

  • Unwanted Direct Messages. This includes:
    • Messaging Staff/Server members for help
    • Advertisements
    • Scams

Help

Helping others is a very large part of our communities, but certain guidelines should be followed when asking for help:

  • Asking:
    • Search online first
    • Avoid Direct Messages
    • No homework/test material
    • Properly Format the question
    • Do not drown other quetions out
    • Large text files are generally not appreciated

  • Helping:
    • Don't Spoonfeed
    • No Insulting
    • Scams

General

Some general guidelines:

  • No NSFW posts
  • No illegal stuff
  • No insults/racial slurs
  • Try to keep it kid friendly
